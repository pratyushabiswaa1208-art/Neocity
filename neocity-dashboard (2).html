United Nations · 2030 Agenda
Sustainable Development Goals
The UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals form the blueprint for a better world by 2030. NeoCity is built around four of the most critical goals — every building, policy, and infrastructure choice you make in the game is a reflection of real-world SDG action.
🏙️
SDG 11
Sustainable Cities & Communities
Make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable for all people.
Key targets in NeoCity
Ensure access to affordable housing and basic services for all citizens.
Provide inclusive, accessible green public spaces for every district.
Reduce the environmental impact of cities through smart urban planning.
Affects: Housing · Transit · Public Space
🌍
SDG 13
Climate Action
Take urgent action to combat climate change and its devastating impacts.
Key targets in NeoCity
Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards.
Integrate climate change measures into city policies and planning.
Reduce carbon emissions through green energy and low-carbon transport.
Affects: Emissions · Resilience · Green Score
⚡
SDG 7
Affordable & Clean Energy
Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.
Key targets in NeoCity
Achieve universal access to affordable and reliable energy services.
Substantially increase the share of renewable energy in the city's mix.
Double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency by 2030.
Affects: Power Grid · Solar · Wind · Efficiency
🔬
SDG 9
Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure
Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive industrialisation and foster innovation.
Key targets in NeoCity
Develop quality, reliable, and resilient infrastructure for all districts.
Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialisation across economic zones.
Increase access to technology, research, and internet connectivity citywide.
Affects: Research · Infrastructure · Tech Level
Source: United Nations SDG Platform · All 17 goals at sdgs.un.org
